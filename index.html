BlackJack Project for CodeWorks

by Padraig O'Donoghue

Welcome to my little Blackjack application!

It won't fool anyone into thinking they're in Las Vegas, it doesn't even contain most of the features you'd expect, it's just a simple project to illustrate my learning and progression in web development.

The rules are simple; hit 'New Game' to play, 'Hit' to get another card and 'Stand' to let the Dealer take over. Remember, you're playing to beat the dealer without exceeding 21.

Good luck!
